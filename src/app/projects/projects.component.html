The Mentorship system would provide students, and mentors the necessary platform to
create and manage appointments. It would as such, provide a comprehensive structure of
data storage. This enabling the superuser (facilitator) to:
Actively track users’ active use of the system.
Monitor the impact mentorship has had on students’ academic performances.
Provide a tools and functionalities required to demonstrate and report issues, or pain points
that exist within modules.
